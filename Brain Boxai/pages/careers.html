JOIN THE TEAM

    Our WFA policy gives you the freedom to work anywhere in the world, with the goal of improving work-life balance. We foster a healthy lifestyle that empowers our team to be autonomous and flexible. The office isn’t for everyone all the time, and we trust that our team members do their best work in their favourite environments.

    Our expanded healthcare plan includes telemedicine so medical care is available when, how, and where you need it. We aim to be flexible in our health support so you can make the best decisions for your wellbeing.     We believe in continuous learning and helping our team members reach their personal and professional goals, through mentorship relationships with colleagues, lunch-and-learns, and more. BrainBox AI is a haven for camaraderie, creativity, and interdepartmental collaboration.

    As globetrotters from over 15 countries, we appreciate and learn from each other. Our diverse team of top-tier professionals offers endless opportunities to learn from each other’s skills, backgrounds, languages, perspectives, and differences.

    We reinvented corporate town halls, making ours an engaging and often humorous time and place to wrap up the week as a team. We believe the whole company should work as one cohesive unit.
